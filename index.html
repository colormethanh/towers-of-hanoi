Towers of Hanoi

A simple game with a long history.

The goal of Towers of Hanoi is to move a set of disk of different sizes from one peg to another.

  • Hover over an item to see it outlined.
  • Select the first peg to move from by clicking it (It will be highlighted in green after clicking).
  • Click another peg to move the disk onto it.
  • If you change your mind click on the highlighted peg again to deselect it.
  • Winner is selected automatically but you can click the check winner button just check.
